JoJoStands: FanStands is a mod implementing the stands of people who have created their own custom Stands! Contributor Dev-Stands are implemented in this mod.

Want to commission your own Stand into the mod?

Join the JoJoStands Discord (https://discord.gg/EYKBhpK) and submit a fanstand commission request!

Warning: Attempting to play this mod in multiplayer can cause a few or many issues with some Stands!
